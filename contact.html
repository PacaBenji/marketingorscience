Editorial Inquiries

For questions about our reviews, methodology, or editorial decisions, please reach the editorial team at info@marketingorscience.com.

We read every message but cannot guarantee a personal response to every inquiry. For questions likely to be useful to other readers, we may publish a response in our Corrections or FAQ section.

Corrections & Feedback

If you believe a published article contains a factual error or misrepresents a study's findings, please see our Corrections Policy for instructions on how to submit a correction request. We take accuracy seriously and respond to all substantive correction requests.

Other Correspondence

For research submission proposals, see our Submit a Proposal page. For press or licensing inquiries, contact info@marketingorscience.com with the subject line "Press" or "Licensing."

We aim to respond to time-sensitive matters within five business days.
