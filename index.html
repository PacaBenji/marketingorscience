Our Mission

Product claims move faster than peer review. We slow it down.

Marketing or Science examines the clinical evidence behind consumer health products — skincare formulations, OTC drugs, and supplements. Every breakdown is structured around the same framework: the Claim, the Evidence, and the Verdict. No brand partnerships. No affiliate revenue. No softened conclusions.

Our readership is health professionals and informed consumers who understand that "clinically tested" is not the same as "clinically proven."
